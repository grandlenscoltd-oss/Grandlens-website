01. About Grandlens

More than a printer.
A brand partner.

"If you can think it,
we can ink it."

Grandlens Company is Nairobi's trusted printing, branding and graphics design studio. Based in Kenbanco House, Nairobi CBD, we've spent over a decade helping businesses — from startups to corporates — look professional, credible, and unforgettable.

Trusted by Kenya Power, NCCK, County Assembly of Turkana, Airtel, Subaru, Madison Insurance and hundreds of Nairobi businesses. When it needs to look right, they call Grandlens.

🏆
Quality First
Every order goes through quality checks before dispatch. We don't release work we're not proud of.
⚡
Fast Turnaround
Same-day banners. 24-hour business cards. 48-hour t-shirts. We work at the speed of Nairobi business.
🎨
In-House Design
No outsourcing. Our designers work with you from brief to final proof before a single sheet is printed.
📦
Delivery Available
Nairobi CBD delivery for orders above KSh 2,000. Countrywide courier delivery also available.
